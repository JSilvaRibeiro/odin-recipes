Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Enjoy a quick and delicious breakfast with this simple scrambled eggs
recipe. These fluffy and flavorful scrambled eggs require minimal
ingredients and take just a few minutes to prepare. Perfect for a wholesome start to your day!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Optional: Chopped herbs (e.g., chives, parsley) for garnish

Steps:

  1. Crack Eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Beat Eggs: Use a fork or whisk to beat the eggs until the yolks and whites are well combined.
  3. Preheat Pan: Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter and let it melt, ensuring the pan is evenly coated.
  4. Pour Eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the preheated pan.
  5. Stir Constantly: With a spatula, gently stir the eggs in a circular motion, ensuring even cooking.
  6. Cook to Desired Consistency: Continue stirring until the eggs are cooked to your desired level of doneness. For creamy scrambled eggs, remove them from the heat just before they are fully set, as they will continue cooking with residual heat.
  7. Season: Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve: Transfer the scrambled eggs to a plate. Garnish with chopped herbs if desired.
  9. Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy your perfectly scrambled eggs!
